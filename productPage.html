The Lyceum Theatre in London is a long and complex tale of success, downfall and rebirth. It all started in 1772 when the Society of Arts founded a room for exhibitions and concerts near the site of the current building. Since this beginning the Lyceum Theatre has displayed a chameleon tendency, adapting to changing fashions and needs admirably.

Now home to Disney’s ‘The Lion King,’ an average 760,000 patrons enjoy the Lyceum’s hospitality every year. You’ll find the Lyceum Theatre stood grandly in the heart of London’s West End, just minutes away from Covent Garden. Buy your Lyceum tickets from the official online box office today.
